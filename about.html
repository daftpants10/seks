Be Hear Now

A place to record what you're hearing. Not to perform it.

What it is
Hold to record. Release to post. Your friends hear it in their feed, chronologically. No algorithm, no likes. Just sound and time.
Echo
Instead of a like, you echo. Hold longer to echo deeper. The ring that forms shows how much something moved you — not just that it did.
To get in
We'll give you a clue. You go out and record it. That recording is your key.
Who it's for
Listeners. People who feel deeply about sound and have no good place to put that. Not as creators. Not as critics. Just as people who are paying attention.
