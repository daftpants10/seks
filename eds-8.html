The Lotus

The Stick — Movement Practice
Put on the music. Pick up the stick. Begin to move freely. You are looking for the movement that requires the least effort to sustain. Not the most impressive, not the most complex: the one that almost moves itself. Try everything. When you find a movement that asks nothing of you to continue, stay with it. That is the lotus. Every body finds a different one.
The Room
A room that only works when many people are in it together. The sticks connect, literally or digitally. The collective holding of the sticks generates the experience. Alone, nothing happens.
The Principle in Action
Stay awake. Build stuff. Help out.
