The Lotus

The Stick — Movement Practice
Find the move with the stick that your body could do forever. There is no instruction here — it is different for every body. Explore. Try things. When you arrive at the move that disappears into you, when you stop noticing you are moving — stay. You have entered satori. This is the lotus.
This is not a technique. It is a homecoming. The lotus move is the one that the body has always known. You are not learning it — you are remembering it. Stay awake. Build stuff. Help out. This is the byte to remember: the game was never something you had to work at. It was always already here.
The Room
A room that only works when many people are in it together. The sticks connect, literally or digitally. The collective holding of the sticks generates the experience. Alone, nothing happens.
The Principle in Action
The circle in the middle of the lotus is the original synapse — the single point of awakening. By EDS 8 that synapse has become a synergy. The human game is a multiplayer game. This room is the endgame of the journey.
