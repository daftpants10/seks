"Welcome to the human game. The goal of the game is to remember that you're playing the game."

The Infinity

The Stick — Movement Practice
Hold the stick in one hand, horizontal. Let the music start. Picture an invisible pane of glass in front of you and try to keep the stick flush to it as you move: slide it left, right, up, down without letting it tip off the surface. Feel the beat as the rhythm that tells you when to shift. Once you have the vertical plane, find the horizontal one (like a table), then the oblique. Each one is a dimension you are learning to read. Name them as the music moves you: X. Y. Z.
The Room
A dark room. One source of light. You find the stick on a plinth. You pick it up. Something changes.
The Principle in Action
The moment of picking up the stick is the moment of waking up. 0 becomes 1. The game begins — or rather, you remember it already did.
