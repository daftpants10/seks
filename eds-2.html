Navigate the Game

The Stick — Movement Practice
Place the stick vertical on your open palm. Find the point of balance. Do not grip — let it rest. All five senses switch on simultaneously. Your eyes, your skin, your inner ear, your breath. Something in you wakes up to manage this one thing.
Balance is navigation. Every micro-adjustment is information arriving through the body. You are not holding the stick — the stick is teaching you how to read the present moment on all channels at once.
The Room
Five zones in the room. Each zone activates one sense through the stick. Light, tone, texture, scent, taste marker. The stick is the key that unlocks each zone.
The Principle in Action
1 fires into 5. The self deploys its instruments. These are the 5 channels through which the multidimensional game is experienced in 3D.
