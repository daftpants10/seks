# seks · north star

*the one paragraph everything hangs beneath*

---

## the statement

> seks gathers people around flow. we turn that flow into art.
> every experience we make leaves behind a piece of collective art —
> a living thing the crowd creates just by moving through it.
> the stick is one way in. loob is where it all remembers itself.

---

## the layers

- **the space** — a flow space. community of people who move.
- **the material** — flow itself: the body, the nervous system, the movement.
- **the output** — art made *from* that flow.
- **the pattern** — every experience produces a collective artwork
  the crowd co-creates by participating.

## what's the actual IP

not the stick. not the sensor. the **format**:

> every seks experience generates a living collective artwork,
> and loob is where those artworks and their memories live.

the stick (⦿-⦿) is the first instrument. the projection sculpture is the
first canvas. but the repeatable thing — the thing you tour, license, and
build the brand on — is **collective flow art**: a new interaction designed
for every space, every festival, every room.
