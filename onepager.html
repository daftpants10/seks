BeReal
for Audio

Once a day, you record 60 seconds of whatever you're hearing or feeling. No editing. Just you and a mic.

Music social media is all performance. Polished clips, curated playlists, aesthetic grids. Nobody shares the actual experience of being a music fan: the late-night rabbit hole, the song you've played 40 times this week, the half-formed feeling you can't name yet. This is the anti-curation layer. A journal, not a stage.

How it works

  1. Open the app, hit record, 60 seconds max
  2. Capture whatever: what you're listening to, a voice memo of a feeling, ambient sound, a hum
  3. It posts to your feed, chronologically
  4. Your friends hear it

Open questions

  • What's the interaction mechanic? If there are no likes or comments, what makes you feel heard?
  • What does your own history look like? That timeline might be the most compelling feature.
  • What gets someone to hit record for the first time?
