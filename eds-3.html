The Code

The Stick — Movement Practice
Hold the stick with one hand at each end. Choose one end to fix, commit to keeping it still in space as if pinned to an invisible point. With your other hand, let the free end move with the music: arcs, circles, figures of eight. The fixed end does not move. The free end follows the sound. Do this until the fixed and the free feel like two separate instruments playing the same track. Then swap.
The Room
A room that responds to movement history. The longer you stay, the more the room reveals. The stick logs your presence and triggers epigenetic sound/light sequences.
The Principle in Action
The more levels you remember, the more fun and consequential the game becomes.
